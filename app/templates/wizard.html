Complete Your Profile

Your profile helps us understand how you use products so we can match you to the right trials.


1. Interests

The product categories and technologies you care about and want to trial. This shows us your willingness to try different things and helps us avoid inviting you to trials that you aren’t interested in.

2. Basic Profile

High-level information that helps us understand who you are as a user — such as usage context, experience level, and general preferences. In other words, this is the stuff you use, and how you see yourself using it.

3. Advanced Profile

This more specific details about you. It includes things like head size, hairstyle, and other factors that help us ensure proper fit and comfort during trials.

You can stop at any time and return later. Your progress will be saved automatically.

Completing your profile does not guarantee selection for every trial, but it helps ensure fair and accurate matching.
